Domain Maps

Giving you just enough vocabulary to go from vague idea to specific prompt

The biggest bottleneck to good AI output isn't the AI — it's that you don't know the words. Domain Maps gives you the terminology to ask for exactly what you're imagining.

The idea

If you don't know what a "Dutch angle" is, you can't ask for one. If you don't know what "cove lighting" means, you'll just say "make it cozy." The AI knows these words — you just need to know them too.

Each map covers a field people are curious about but typically lack formal training in. Structured as domains → branches → categories → terms. Deliberately not exhaustive. Think 10 terms per category — enough to go from vague to specific, not so many it becomes a textbook.
